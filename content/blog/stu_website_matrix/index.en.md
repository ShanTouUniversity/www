ST.U (ShanTou.University) is an unofficial Shantou University alumni community platform. In addition to this main blog, we operate a series of sub-sites that together form the ST.U website matrix.

## Website Matrix Overview

| Site | Domain | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Main | [shantou.university](https://shantou.university) | ST.U main blog with articles and projects |
| Nav | [nav.shantou.university](https://nav.shantou.university) | STU常用网站导航，汇集校园服务与工具 |
| News | [news.shantou.university](https://news.shantou.university) | Unofficial campus news platform |
| Voice | [voice.shantou.university](https://voice.shantou.university) | Student voice platform |
| Space | [space.shantou.university](https://space.shantou.university) | Tech blog and creative space |

## Site Details

### Main Site shantou.university

The core ST.U site publishing articles, guides, and projects about Shantou University:

- Campus guides
- Academic resources
- Alumni stories
- Tech sharing

### Nav nav.shantou.university

A navigation hub gathering commonly used STU websites and services:

- Office systems
- Academic units
- Learning services
- Campus services
- Useful tools
- Learning resources
- Campus life
- Entertainment

### News news.shantou.university

An unofficial campus news platform with bilingual (Chinese/English) content:

- Campus news
- Notices
- Academic research
- Student activities
- Profiles
- Media coverage

### Voice voice.shantou.university

"Student Voice" - a platform for STU students to freely express their thoughts. Built with Zola, featuring a clean and focused theme.

### Space space.shantou.university

A tech blog and creative space sharing technical articles and various interesting projects, featuring a graffiti wall and other interactive features.

## Open Source

All source code is hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/ShanTouUniversity). Contributions via Issues and Pull Requests are welcome.
